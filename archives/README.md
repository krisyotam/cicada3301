# Primary Source Archives — Community Records & Documentation

Original community-maintained archives capturing puzzle materials, solving discussions, wiki documentation, and media coverage. These archives represent the historical record of Cicada 3301 across multiple platforms.

## Contents

- **cijhho/** — Original puzzle files, community discussions, and metadata from early solving efforts. Named after an early archiver
- **scream314/** — Year-by-year markdown documentation and structured puzzle analysis
- **wiki/** — Pages from the Uncovering Cicada Fandom Wiki (129+ pages covering Gematria Primus, tools, poster locations, and unsolved leads)
- **irc-logs/** — Historical IRC discussions from #cicadasolvers on Libera.Chat and other networks documenting real-time solving progress
- **media-coverage/** — Journalism and documentary materials (Rolling Stone, NPR, CBC, LEMMiNO documentary, etc.)

## Significance

These archives preserve the collaborative solving process that unfolded across Discord, Reddit, IRC, and other platforms. They document the progression from initial mystery through partial solution of Liber Primus.
