# Liber Primus — The Unsolved Book

The 58-page runic codex released as the culmination of the 2014 Cicada 3301 puzzle. Approximately 17 pages have been decoded; the remaining 41 pages remain unsolved. Contains esoteric philosophical text rendered entirely in Elder Futhark runes using the Gematria Primus cipher system.

## Key Statistics

- Released: May 2, 2014
- Pages: 58
- Solved: ~17 pages (last 2 were among first solved)
- Unsolved: ~41 pages

## Decryption Methods Used

Solved pages employed combinations of Gematria Primus transliteration, Vigenère ciphers with prime-based keys, Euler's totient function, and Atbash variants. The remaining pages suggest multi-layered encryption or a fundamentally different cipher system.
