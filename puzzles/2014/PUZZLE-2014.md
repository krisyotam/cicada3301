# 2014 Puzzle — The Third Round & Liber Primus

**Posted**: January 4, 2014 (Twitter @1231507051321)

## Overview

The third and final known puzzle round culminated in the release of **Liber Primus** — a 58-page book of Elder Futhark runes that remains largely unsolved to this day.

## Initial Clue

A fresh clue was posted on the official Twitter account [@1231507051321](https://x.com/1231507051321), beginning the third puzzle chain.

## Liber Primus

On **May 2, 2014**, Liber Primus was released with the message:

> Hello. Your enlightenment awaits you. We look forward to hearing from you.

The book consists of 58 pages of text rendered entirely in Elder Futhark runes, accompanied by various symbols and imagery. It is the central unsolved artifact of Cicada 3301.

See: [liber-primus/](../../liber-primus/) for full documentation.

## Post-2014 Communications

### July 27, 2015
PGP-signed Pastebin statement posted via @1231507051321 denying involvement in the Planned Parenthood hack. This confirmed the PGP key was still in active use.

### January 5, 2016
New Twitter post with image on Infotomb, encouraging continued Liber Primus decoding work.

### April 2017
**Final known PGP-signed message**: "Beware false paths. Always verify PGP signature from 7A35090F."

No verified communication has been received since.
